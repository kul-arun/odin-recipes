Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Preparation

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven (350 degrees F).
